1

Welcome & Story Introduction (10 mins)

"Welcome to the Chess Kingdom! Each piece has a special job to protect their king..." Interactive storytelling with visual aids and student participation.

2

Piece Discovery Workshop (20 mins)

Hands-on exploration of each piece with memory games, pattern recognition, and "piece personality" activities. Students create their own chess character stories.

3

Kingdom Building Challenge (15 mins)

"Set up your kingdom!" - Students arrange pieces while explaining roles. Includes team-building exercises and friendly competitions.

4

Strategic Thinking Games (10 mins)

Simple chess-based puzzles and logic games to reinforce piece knowledge while developing critical thinking skills.

5

Reflection & Goal Setting (5 mins)

Student shares discoveries, celebrates progress, and sets personalized goals for next session. Includes take-home practice activities.
