Our Method

Learning That Sticks for Life

Every session is carefully structured to challenge each student at their own level. Truth's coaching method blends storytelling, game analysis, and real-time feedback — so students build genuine understanding, not just memorized moves.

Lessons are held virtually via video call with interactive digital boards, making world-class instruction accessible from anywhere in the DC Metro area and beyond.
