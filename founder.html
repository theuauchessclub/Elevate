The Story Behind the Vision

Truth Hall's journey into chess education began not in a classroom, but on the streets of his childhood neighborhood, where he discovered that chess was more than a game—it was a pathway to transformation.

Growing up in an underserved community, Truth witnessed firsthand how strategic thinking could change a young person's trajectory. He watched peers who struggled academically suddenly excel when they learned to think several moves ahead on the chessboard.

This revelation sparked a lifelong mission: to bring the cognitive and character-building benefits of chess to every child, regardless of their background or circumstances.
